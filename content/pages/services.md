## 1:1 Coaching
### Online Coaching — £120 / month

**Online Coaching** is for lifters who want a highly individualised offer, in which I provide consistent feedback and support. I can help you whether you’re preparing for your first meet or pushing for a new total. This is coaching that is tailored to your goals, experience level, and day-to-day life.

You’ll get fully customised programming designed to build strength and develop technique, with weekly check-ins to review progress and adjust your plan as needed. Video feedback helps dial in technique to ensure you’re training effectively and safely.

For those competing under British Powerlifting, I also offer in-depth competition planning and strategy. We’ll cover attempt selection, timing, rules, and meet-day logistics so you feel confident stepping onto the platform and can focus on performing at your best.

*Note: This is not recommended for absolute beginners - if you have no experience in strength training, I suggest starting with an in-person session or hybrid coaching.*

**What’s included:**

- Fully individualised programme delivered via the MyStrengthBook app
- Weekly check-ins to review progress and adjust your plan
- Video technique feedback
- Competition planning and meet-day strategy
- Support and accountability to keep you on track

[**Apply for Online Coaching**](../contact)

**How it works:**

1. Get in touch
2. We talk through your goals - via zoom/email/whatsapp, whatever works for you
3. You start training!

### Hybrid Coaching — £200 / month

**Hybrid Coaching** is for lifters who want the best of both worlds: the flexibility and personalised programming of online coaching, along with personal support and focussed technique work in person. This is ideal for you if:

- you want to build strength and improve your technique
- you are preparing for a competition and want to dial in your performance
- you are a new lifter who wants to get the basics nailed down from the start

It includes everything in the Online Coaching package, plus two 1h in-person sessions per month.

[**Apply for Hybrid Coaching**](../contact)
